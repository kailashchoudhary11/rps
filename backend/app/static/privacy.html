Privacy Policy

Pic Studios · Effective 5 May 2026

This policy explains how the Pic Studios app handles your information. Pic Studios is a photo-sharing app for event attendees: the photo studio running your event gives you a short event code, you type it into the app, and the app shows you the photos from that event so you can view and save them.

Information we collect from you

We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, age, location, or any other personal details. The app has no sign-up and no user accounts.

The only thing the app sends to us is the event code you type in, which we use to look up the photos for that event. The code is not associated with you or your device.

What we don't collect or do

  • We don't run analytics that record what you tap or how long you stay.
  • We don't access your contacts, location, microphone, or camera.
  • We don't read or upload your existing photos or media.
  • We don't ask you to create an account, sign in, or share an email.

Photos in the app

The photos shown in the app are uploaded by the photo studio running your event. They are loaded onto your phone only while you are viewing the gallery.

When you tap the download button, that photo is saved to your phone's own Photos / Gallery app, inside an album called Pic Studios. Saved photos stay on your device only — the app does not upload them or share them anywhere.

Permissions the app asks for

The app uses two device permissions, each only for what they sound like:

  • Internet access — needed to look up your event and load its photos. The app cannot work offline.
  • Permission to save photos — used only when you tap the download button, so the photo can land in your gallery. We do not read or send your existing photos with this permission.

Sharing your information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user information with anyone. Because we do not collect personal information from you in the first place, there is nothing to sell or share.

How long information is kept

Each event has its own lifetime, set by the photo studio that created it — either until the studio deletes the event, or until an expiry date the studio chose. We keep no record of who entered an event code, when, or from which device, so there is no per-user history to retain or delete.

Security

All communication between the app and our servers uses secure, encrypted connections. Because the app has no accounts and no passwords, there are no login credentials to protect.

Children

Pic Studios is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has shared personal information with us through the app, please contact us so we can address it.

Your choices

You can uninstall the app at any time to remove its access to your device. Photos already saved to your gallery stay where they are — the app no longer controls them once they're saved.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Material changes will be reflected in the date at the top of the page.
