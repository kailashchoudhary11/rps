Privacy Policy

Pic Studios · Effective 5 May 2026

Overview

Pic Studios is a mobile app that lets event guests view and download their event photos using a short event code provided by the photo studio. This policy describes what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information we collect from users

None directly. The app does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, phone number, or location, and does not include analytics or advertising SDKs. No device identifiers are collected.

Information transmitted by the app

When you enter an event code and tap Access Photos, the app sends that code to our backend server to look up the corresponding event and the list of available photos. No other information is transmitted from the app.

Photo bytes are loaded directly from a private Cloudflare R2 storage bucket using short-lived, signed URLs that the backend issues per request. Photo data flows from Cloudflare R2 to your device; it is not routed through our backend.

What we store on our servers

Our backend stores event metadata only — the event code, event name, studio name, creation date, and an optional expiry date. Photographs are held in a private Cloudflare R2 bucket controlled by the studio. We do not log which device requested which event, nor do we keep any history of access.

What the app stores on your device

Photos that you tap the download button on are saved to your device's photo gallery, inside an album named Pic Studios. They are saved on-device only. The app does not transmit any of your existing photos or media anywhere.

Permissions the app requests

  • Internet — to fetch event information and load event photos.
  • Photos / Media access (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES on Android 13+, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE on Android 9 and earlier) — required only when you choose to download a photo, so the app can save it to your gallery.

The app does not access your existing photos or media in any other way.

Third-party services

The only external service the app communicates with is Cloudflare R2, which hosts the photo files. Cloudflare's privacy practices are described at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy. No advertising, analytics, attribution, crash-reporting, or other third-party SDKs are integrated into the app.

Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any data to third parties. We have no business relationship that would involve sharing data.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, regardless of age.

Data retention and deletion

Because the app does not collect personal information, there is no personal data to retain or delete. Event metadata stored on our servers is controlled by the studio operating the event and is removed when the event is deleted or expires.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be available at the same URL with an updated effective date.
